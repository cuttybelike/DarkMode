# PartA: Enable Dark Mode

User wants to enable Dark mode on Windows11 but not sure how to change setting

## Troubleshoot inquires

- Do you have any experience with a computer?
- Have you ever used Windows11 before?
- When was the last time the computer was updated?
- How old is your computer?
- What year did you purchase your computer?
- Have you ever made any hardware changes to the computer, whether yourself or technician or both?
